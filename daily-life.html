Artificial Intelligence (AI) and its multiple sub-domains are being increasingly employed in various industries and businesses to aid in repetitive processes. But there has been a burgeoning interest from established tech giants and startups in using AI to make everyday life a walk in the park.

Examples of AI in daily life

  • Smart assistants: Voice assistants like Siri, Alexa, and Google Assistant can perform tasks based on voice commands and user preferences.
  • Image recognition: AI can recognize faces for mobile phone unlocking.
  • Fraud prevention: AI can detect financial fraud.
  • Email filtering: AI can filter emails.
  • Language translation: AI can translate languages.
  • Navigation: AI can help with navigation and maps.
  • Social media: AI can monitor social media and analyze social media data.
  • Online shopping: AI can provide personalized recommendations for online shopping.
  • Chatbots: AI-powered chatbots can interact with users in natural language.
  • Text editing: AI can help with text editing and autocorrect.

Benefits of AI

  1. AI can help people do more work by replacing tedious or dangerous tasks.
  2. AI can free up people to focus on tasks that require creativity and empathy.
  3. AI can help financial institutions personalize services and products, manage risk, and automate operations.

AI can also be used in healthcare management, manufacturing, and transportation

    The implementation of cloud technology and the ability to use data at scale has been generating value in enterprises. Now, adding generative AI to this mix creates a catalyst to dramatically boost productivity and lower costs by assisting human interactions with automation. In healthcare, generative AI (genAI) can create novel experiences, empower organizations to derive meaning and insights from data, and fuel innovation.

    Artificial Intelligence in machine manufacturing is no longer a vision of the future, even though it often still appears that way. In fact, AI solutions already play a crucial role today in optimizing processes at various levels. Many companies worldwide have successfully integrated AI into their operations, particularly in manufacturing, setting new benchmarks for innovation and efficiency.

    Artificial intelligence (AI) is used in transportation to improve safety, efficiency, and traffic management. AI can be used in many ways, including traffic management, autonomous vehicles, and predictive maintenance.
